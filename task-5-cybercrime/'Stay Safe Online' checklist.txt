
 'Stay Safe Online' checklist 

Use strong, unique passwords for each account (at least 12 characters with mix of letters, numbers, symbols).
Turn on 2-Factor Authentication (2FA) for email, social media, and banking apps. 
Never share OTPs or passwords—even with someone claiming to be from your bank or college.
Install apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store. 
 
Regularly update your phone, apps, and antivirus to fix security vulnerabilities. 

Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks (banking, payments, logins).
Do not scan random QR codes to receive money—QR scans are usually for sending money. 

Always double-check UPI ID / recipient name before sending money.


Report Cybercrime:
1. National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
2. Helpline Number: 1930
